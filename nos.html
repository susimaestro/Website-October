Tools

Google forms, Adobe XD, Photoshop & Illustrator

Research

The research can be divided into four categories; Desk, User, Market and Competitive research. My focus was to get to know NOS and their users to suss out pain points, frustrations and room for improvement. Through the user and competitive research I could get a better idea of the users expectation of a streaming service, and also be able to see where the gaps where to brainstorm design opportunities

Since NOS is Portuguese I choose to do the survey in Portuguese to make sure there wouldn't be any confusion or lack of expression because of a language barrier. At this stage I wanted to know what NOS services they used, what services they use to stream movies and shows and also what they like and don't like about NOS Play. 72 people participated in the survey.

The survey revealed that almost 80% of the NOS customers use Netlix to stream movies and shows, and 72% use HBO. According to my survey only 20% of NOS customers use NOS Play. 2/9 interviewees said they use NOS Play once a month, otherwise they use Netflix and HBO daily.

I started looking into their competition to find out what they did well and to see if there was something they could improve on.

In the interviews and surveys 80% said that they gave up on using NOS Play because they found the interface confusing to use. To dig into this deeper I thought it could be good to do a UX audit in form of giving tasks to first time users and see how they perform on the website. I did 5 user testing with simple tasks like: "find any thriller movie" and "navigate from movies to tv shows"
