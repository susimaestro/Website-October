FRIENDREC:
Turns your friend's recommendations
into a travel guide

This is a personal project of mine. I was going to Porto for the first time and was asking my friends what I should see, eat and drink and got loads of great recommendations from them. The day before I was trying to piece together all the information I got from friends, different social media accounts, restaurant websites, the directions from google maps etc. and got quite overwhelemed. It would be great to only have to refer to one single source on my trip!

Problem

When you travel and get your travel tips partly from that website, that friend and that social media account it can be difficult to have a clear overview

Goal

To find a way to organize all the information neatly into one application

Solution

A mobile app that organizes your friends recommendations into categories so you have everything you need for you trip in one place
